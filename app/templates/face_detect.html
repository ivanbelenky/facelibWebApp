Face detection

Face detection is the first step used by fa¢age to perform an analysis on the picture uploaded by the users. It crops out and segregates the faces present in the image. It also finds the markers for each face on the picture.

Face markers

Face markers indicate the position of both eyes (if visible), the tip of the nose and the left and right end of the mouth. Those are used for alignment and enable correct cropping.

The NN architecture used

Face segmentation is created with a pretrained MobileNet, architectures that as the name suggests, has mobile and embedded vision applications in mind. MobileNets are based on a streamlined architecture that uses depth-wise separable convolutions to build light weight deep NN. They efficiently trade off between latency and accuracy allowing in consequence the model builder to choose the right sized model for their application based on the constraints of the problem. It is the case that the backbone library used for this webapp has identical capabilities in real-time video.
